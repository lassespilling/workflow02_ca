Why do we need them?

Energy is stored as fat in our bodies, but some nutrients called essential fatty acids are not self-manufactured so foods containing dietary fat must be consumed. Dietary fat is important to bodybuilders because it helps keep the blood flowing, the heart beating correctly and keeps the skin and hair healthy. 1
