So you might ask:

Do we even need them?

For vegetarians, especially those who work out, taking that extra step to make sure you're adding essential amino acids to your diet is paramount. After all, cutting meat from your diet comes at a cost, but that doesn't have to be your gains. Check out this list of pre- and post-workout supplements to help you fuel your workouts and reach your full fitness potential. 1

Creatine Beta-Alanine BCAA Glutamine

Creatine Monohydrate

only get through diet

  • Good for: storing energy

  • Daily dose 5 g

Beta-Alanine

produced in muscle tissue

  • Good for: delay fatigue

  • Daily dose 3 - 6 g

BCAAs

Drink before, during, and after workouts

  • Good for: suppress protein degradation

  • Daily dose 10 - 15 g

Glutamine

Drink 10g before, and 10g after workouts

  • Good for: Supports immune system, regulate acid-base balance

  • Daily dose 20 g
